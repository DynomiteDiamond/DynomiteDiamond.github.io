Splatoon 2

What is splatoon 2?

Splatoon 2 is a video game for the nintendo switch of the genre "3rd person shooter". You play as squids who shoot ink out of squirt guns

In the game, you can:

  • Battle against other players
  • Complete the story mode
  • Co-operate with other players against enemies
